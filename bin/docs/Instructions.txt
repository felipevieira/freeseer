These instructions are meant for using Freesseer
to record a conference (in FOSSLC-typical configuration).

1) Run Freeseer
2) Click on the configure tab
3) Click the Hardware radio button
4) Click on the main tab
5) Pick the right talk
6) When the speaker is ready to go, click record, wave them to start
once the video starts displaying under the Title dropdown.
7) Click stop once the questions are wrapped up


a) Please get the speaker to complete a release form. It's fine if they opt out, in those cases, we'll simply not record.
b) Please ask the speaker to repeat the questions for the video.
